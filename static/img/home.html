“Barrister Dairy” is used for the purpose of maintaining the details of the clients which are necessary through different stages. This project initially will generate a client’s case ID, which will pursue the entire client’s case details like date of case filed, status of the current case, whether the fee amount is cleared or not, personal details of the clients, necessary witness required etc, can be fed to the software and all the details are available at the finger tips of the user.
